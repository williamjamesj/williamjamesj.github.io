Types of Encryption

Caesar Cipher

The first method (and most basic) method of encryption is the Caesar Cipher, a method that simply shifts all of the characters through the alphabet by a certain number (the key). For example, the plaintext "hello" shifted by a key of 1 would become "ifmmp", which becomes unreadable unless decrypted.
